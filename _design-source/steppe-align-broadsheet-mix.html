The Charter

Article I · Entrenched

No advertising, ever

Member dues pay for Steppe, so it works for members instead of advertisers. The articles forbid changing that.

Article II · Entrenched

Verify, then forget

Joining means proving you live here. After that, the proof is deleted — local membership without tracking.

Article III · Entrenched

Members govern it

One member, one vote. Members set the budget, elect the board, and can recall it. No founder gets the last word.

Article IV · Entrenched

Your data is yours

Take everything you've added and leave whenever you want. Never sold; can't be bought by anyone who would.
