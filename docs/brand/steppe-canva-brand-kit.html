Everything organized to Canva's Brand Hub. Upload the logo and element files, type the colors and fonts into the matching fields, and paste the voice notes. Section headings below match Canva's own.

Brand voice

Brand Hub → Brand Voice. Paste these as the guidance.

Sound like

  • Plain, declarative, grounded in Central Oregon.
  • Warm, sturdy, civic — a neighbor, not a startup.
  • Specific and concrete over abstract.
  • Always credit local artists; never overclaim.

Avoid

  • Contrastive "not X — Y" reframes.
  • Negative triads ("no ads, no tracking, no…").
  • "By design," and hype words.
  • Heavy em-dashes and breathless tone.
06

Usage

Clear space & size

  • Keep at least half the seal's diameter clear around the lockup.
  • Seal no smaller than 20px; lockup wordmark no smaller than 16px.

The ring

  • Juniper ring on light grounds.
  • Bone ring on juniper, basalt, or night grounds (use the on-dark files).

Don't

  • Recolor the strata bands or the sun.
  • Rotate the seal or add shadows/effects.
  • Set the wordmark in any face but Besley.

Backgrounds

  • Paper or bone for light layouts; juniper or basalt-deep for dark.
  • Rust is an accent — small doses, not fields.
